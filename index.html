THE ORIGINAL

1970

As the U.S. landfill crisis escalates, we launch our very first product: Natural, biodegradable laundry detergent that gets refilled, not replaced, when done.

1975

Meet THE first-ever natural toothpaste. It was very different! It was made with coconut oil and flavored with herbs and fennel. 40 years later it’s still our #1 seller.

1990

"Let's start donating our profits to good causes!" we think. It starts with 5%, soon becomes 7% and then 10%.

1995

They said it was impossible. We did it. We're the first ever Natural Toothpaste certified by the ADA.

2020

IntroducinG the recyclable toothpaste tube! It took five years, countless iterations, and a lot of science. But we did it.
